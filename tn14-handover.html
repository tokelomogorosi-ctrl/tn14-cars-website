Section three · Connecting your platforms

Link everything up.

Your website works on its own, but it works much harder when it is connected to the places people already look for you. Here is what to update and how to do it.

1

Google Business Profile

This is the listing that appears when someone searches "TN14 Cars" or "taxi Sevenoaks" on Google. Adding your website here means anyone who finds you on Google can click straight through.

How to do it:
1. Go to business.google.com and sign in with the Google account linked to TN14 Cars.
2. Click "Edit profile."
3. Find the "Website" field and paste your website address.
4. Click Save. Google updates within a few hours.
2

Facebook page

If TN14 Cars has a Facebook page, adding the website there means anyone who finds you on Facebook can visit the site with one tap.

How to do it:
1. Go to your Facebook page and click "Edit page info" (or the pencil icon).
2. Find the "Website" field and paste your website address.
3. Click Save. Done.
3

WhatsApp Business

Your WhatsApp number is already on the website. If you use WhatsApp Business, add the website to your business profile so it appears when people message you.

How to do it:
1. Open WhatsApp Business, tap the three dots and go to "Business tools."
2. Tap "Business profile" and find the "Website" field.
3. Paste your website address and save.
4

Your email signature

Every email you or your team sends is an opportunity. Add the website link to your email signature so every person you contact can reach the site in one click. Ask your team to do the same.
